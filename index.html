Never Gonna Give you up

    Background
  • This song was July 27, 1987 by Rick Astley and was originally a love song. But the internet back then like the times of 4chan they did duck roll baiting people into clicking a link and showing a duck car thing. And eventually some people thought it would be funny to put the song Never Gonna Give You Up and bait people the same way as the duck roll, and thats how the Rick Roll was born
